Hi! Welcome to my portfolio.

My name is Alice Tran,

I am a computer science major at the University of California Irvine. This website will show some projects of mine and more about who I am.

After taking my first coding class in college, I knew I had found my place. Lately I have been diving deeper into the UX/UI field and found myself more and more passionate for designing. I hope I can do more for others with my designs and also hope that you got to learn a little bit more about me through this portfolio. Creativity has always been my driving force and I have always found myself passionate about arts and designing. One way I am able to creatively express myself is through VenusHacks, a women-centric hackathon in which I am a Marketing Organizer for, specifically in Website and Graphics. Another hobby of mine is building mechanical keyboards. I like being able to put things together and have a final product come out of it which is probably why I find interest in arts. I am able to do more with this hobby as Vice President of Keebs Club @ UCI in which I organize keyboard meets for people who are also interested in this hobby to share this passion of theirs.
